# Cyclic representations of receptor complexes

:::{note}
Receptor oligomers are modeled as identical (but not independent) Markov chains with a state-transition graph that is a reduced Cartesian graph product. Beginning with a minimal cycle basis construction of such graph products, algebraic enumeration of spanning trees gives insight into receptor dimer models, in particular, thermodynamic constraints on allosteric modulation. 
:::
